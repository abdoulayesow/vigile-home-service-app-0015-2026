- Based in South Conroe, TX — suburban Houston area

- Fluent in French and English; deeply connected to francophone West African networks
- Operates multiple side projects (Improve So LLC, AI apps for West African markets)

| Problem | Impact |
|---|---|

### 🏠 Appliances & Machines

|---|---|---|

| GE Dryer | Clean lint trap, clean full vent duct (fire hazard) | Lint trap: every use; duct: 1x/year |
| GE Dishwasher | Clean filter, run cleaning cycle, check spray arms | Monthly |
| Coffee machine | Descale | Every 2–3 months (hard water) |
| Ecovacs Deebot robot vacuum | Clean brushes, empty bin, clean sensors, replace filter | Bin: weekly; deep clean: monthly; filter: every 2–3 months |
| Refrigerator | Clean condenser coils, check door seals | Coils: 2x/year; seals: quarterly |

### 🔋 Safety & Seasonal
| Item | Maintenance | Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| Smoke detectors | Test + replace batteries | Every 6 months (Spring + Fall) |
| CO detectors | Test + replace batteries | Every 6 months |
| Fire extinguisher | Visual inspection, check pressure | 1x/year |
| Hurricane prep kit | Check water, flashlights, documents, supplies | Every May |
| Freeze prep | Locate pipe shutoff, insulate exposed pipes | Every November |

### ⚠️ Blind Spots — What You Probably Haven't Thought About
*Vigile surfaces these at the right time of year*

| Item | Why It Matters in Conroe |
|---|---|
| **Foundation cracks** | South Conroe sits on expansive clay soil — check every Spring and after drought periods |
| **Attic ventilation & insulation** | Houston heat + humidity = moisture buildup and mold risk. Under-insulated attics spike AC bills dramatically |
| **Full dryer vent duct path** | Beyond the lint trap — the full duct to the exterior is a leading house fire cause |
| **Bathroom caulk** | Houston humidity destroys caulk fast. Mold follows caulk failure. Check every 6 months |
| **Electrical panel** | Annual visual check — rust, tripped breakers, burning smell |
| **Exterior faucet backflow preventer** | Inspect annually — protects drinking water, required by code |
| **Flooding / drainage assessment** | South Conroe flooding is real — check yard drainage before hurricane season |

## 4. Product Vision

### Vision Statement

> **For homeowners like Ablo — professionals with high cognitive load and high standards — Vigile is an AI-powered home guardian that delivers proactive, hyper-local, context-aware maintenance briefs directly to their inbox and WhatsApp, before problems happen.**
>
> Unlike generic home apps that require you to show up and check in, Vigile shows up for you — armed with knowledge of your specific home, your climate, your lawn, your appliances, your systems — and tells you exactly what to do and why, this month, right now. It meets you where you already are: email for depth, WhatsApp for immediacy.
>
> **It turns "I should have done that" into "I'm glad I did that."**

### The Shift Vigile Creates

| Before Vigile | After Vigile |
|---|---|
| Pull — you remember to look | Push — it arrives in your inbox and WhatsApp |
| Generic — advice for any home | Specific — your home, your systems, your city |
| Reactive — fix what broke | Proactive — prevent what could break |
| Siloed — only you know | Shared — wife or family looped in via email or WhatsApp |
| Forgotten — until expensive | Timely — 1st of every month, without fail |
| Incomplete — only what you remember | Comprehensive — Vigile flags what you forgot |

### North Star Metric
> **% of maintenance tasks completed BEFORE a problem occurs**
